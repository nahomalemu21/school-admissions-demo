Which grade levels are currently accepting students?+

Applications are open from Kindergarten through Grade 12, subject to availability in each class.

Can parents visit before applying?+

Yes. Campus visits are available during school hours by appointment with the admissions office.

Does the school provide transport?+

Transport is available on selected routes across Addis Ababa. Route details are confirmed during registration.

How will I know my application was received?+

You will see an instant confirmation and receive a follow-up call from the admissions team.
